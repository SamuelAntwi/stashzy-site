Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 February 2026

Stashzy (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Stashzy mobile application and related services (the “App”) handle information when you use Stashzy.

If you have questions, contact us at: appsupport@0224solutions.com

1) What Stashzy does

Stashzy helps you organise and track items in your home inventory (e.g., household items, appliances, wardrobe, supplies), including optional photos, notes, categories, and replacement reminders.

2) Information you provide

  • Inventory content: item names, categories, notes, purchase/acquisition dates, values, quantities, replacement dates, warranty info (if you enter it).
  • Photos: images you take or select to attach to items.
  • Optional metadata: brand, model, serial number, or location within your home (only if you choose to add it).

Important: Stashzy is designed to work without requiring you to create an account (unless you enable optional account/cloud features in a future version).

3) Information we collect automatically

We aim to collect as little as possible. The App may collect limited technical data to keep things working and improve stability, such as device/app information and crash/performance data.

4) How your data is stored

Local-first design: Your inventory data and photos are stored on your device by default.

If Stashzy later offers optional cloud sync/backup, this policy will be updated to clearly explain what data is synced, where it is stored, how it is protected, and how you can disable it.

5) Permissions (Camera / Photos)

Stashzy may request access to Camera (to take item photos) and Photo Library (to select item photos). You can control these permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

6) How we use information

  • Provide core features (inventory tracking, photos, reminders)
  • Maintain app functionality and security
  • Improve performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support if you contact us

7) Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers for app operations (under safeguards), for legal reasons, or during business changes (with notice for material changes).

8) Analytics and tracking

Stashzy is designed to avoid invasive tracking. If tracking requiring consent is introduced, we will request your permission first.

9) Data retention

Because your inventory is stored on-device by default, it remains until you delete it, delete the app, or reset your device. Technical logs (if any) are kept only as long as needed.

10) Your choices and controls

  • Edit or delete inventory items anytime
  • Remove photos from items
  • Delete the App to remove locally stored data
  • Control Camera/Photos permissions in iOS Settings

11) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we aim to use appropriate safeguards.

12) Children’s privacy

Stashzy is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13) International users

If you use Stashzy outside the UK, your information may be processed in the country where you use your device and where our service providers operate (if applicable).

14) Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. We will update the date above and provide notice for material changes.

15) Contact us

Email: appsupport@0224solutions.com
