Support

Help, troubleshooting, and contact details.

Contact

Email: appsupport@0224solutions.com

When contacting support, include

  • Your iPhone model
  • iOS version
  • Stashzy version
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • Screenshots (optional)

Common fixes

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Update to the latest iOS version available
  • Ensure you have free storage space for photos
  • Check Settings → Privacy & Security for Camera/Photos permissions
