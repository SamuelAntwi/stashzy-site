FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Does Stashzy require an account?

No. Stashzy is designed to work without an account by default.

Where is my inventory data stored?

On your device by default (local-first). If cloud sync is added later, the Privacy Policy will be updated.

Does Stashzy sell my data?

No.

Why does Stashzy request Camera/Photos permission?

Only to let you attach photos to items. You can manage permissions in iOS Settings.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete items within the app. To remove all local data, delete the app from your device.

Can I export my inventory?

If export is not available in your current version, it may be added in a future update.
